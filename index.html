I’m a designer with skills in digital photography, print design, and editorial, with a background in illustration and fine art.

On the side, I am also sharpening my dexterity in front-end web design, after school I plan on continuing my education in French and maybe even (finally) wind down to adopt a pooch.

I have a BFA in Graphic Design from Montserrat College of Art near Boston, but my origin story begins in Tampa. In 2018 I interned with a local in-house design studio called Steez Design.

When I’m not creating, I am out hiking, enjoying an iced coffee, or scouring thrift stores in search of more obnoxious windbreakers to add to my collection.
